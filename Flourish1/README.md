## Visualization Overview

This visualization shows the busiest streets in Seattle based on Average Weekday Traffic (AWDT) in 2020. The chart compares traffic volume across selected streets to highlight which areas experience the highest levels of vehicle activity.

The dataset was obtained from the Seattle Open Data Portal (Traffic Flow Count, 2020). The data was cleaned and filtered in Excel to include only the top 10 streets with the highest traffic volumes, making the visualization easier to read and interpret.

One key insight from the visualization is that the West Seattle Bridge carries significantly more traffic than any other street in the dataset, indicating its importance as a major transportation route in the city.
