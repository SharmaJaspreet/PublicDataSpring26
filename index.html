Los Angeles Lakers 2025-26 Season Review

Season Overview

The Lakers had an exciting regular season this year and finished near the top of the Western Conference. The addition of Luka Dončić gave the team another superstar next to LeBron James and helped improve the offense.

One of the biggest strengths of the team was their scoring ability and fast-paced offense. Austin Reaves also had an impressive season and became an important part of the lineup.

Even though the team had strong performances, defense and injuries were sometimes a problem during the season. Overall, the Lakers still looked like a dangerous playoff team.

My Thoughts

I enjoyed watching the Lakers this season because the games were entertaining and the team chemistry improved over time. My favorite part of the season was watching LeBron and Luka play together.

Season Rating: 8.5/10
